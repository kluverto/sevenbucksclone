BREAKING OLYMPIA

AVAILABLE NOW

Phil Heath’s rise to the top of the bodybuilding mountain and the adversity of trying to come all the way back from the two-year layoff because of injuries.

Plagued by injuries that come with 18 years of physical and mental sacrifice, Phil Heath stands on the edge of altering the face of bodybuilding history… “BREAKING OLYMPIA” is the feature documentary unveiling the extraordinary life story of 7-time Mr. Olympia, Phil Heath, as he rises through the ranks, battles the scrutiny of the media, and takes on bodybuilding’s scariest foes in an attempt to reclaim the throne of Mr. Olympia and cement his legacy as one of the greatest bodybuilders to ever walk the earth.

Creators: Brett Harvey, Adam Scorgie. Executive Producers: Dany Garcia, Dwayne Johnson, Erin Lardy, Nikki Cuff, Hiram Garcia, Brian Gewirtz, Kevin Hill.
